Mentorship Program proudly presents the showcase Collective Conscious, our mentorship showcase hosted at New Art City. With the theme of Dreams, each mentor and mentee pairings/trios collaborated on Digital Media art pieces to convey creations that hold meaning to them creatively, aesthetically, and with imagination. A creative and innovative program that helps build and bridge seniors to freshmen and transfers, this showcase is an opportunity for both the mentors and mentees to shine. Visit as digital citizens and enjoy viewing each piece as collaborative entities.
